Epic Weather is an app that tells you what to wear based on current weather conditions.

Smart

We created an algorithm that predicts what kind of outfit would suit the current weather conditions. It can also be tailored to your personal preferences.

You will never see the same clothing combination again. We made sure that there's an infinite amount of possible clothing combos to keep it interesting until the end of time.

Fun

Epic weather is not just a dull platform that tells you what to wear. We created a lot of easter eggs that will randomly appear and make you laugh your ass off.
